Stressless was a prototype smart stress ball I developed in a team in my Master's year at university as part of an Interactive Devices Unit.

We built a device using a regular foam stressball with magnets and Hall Effect Sensors connected to an Arduino board. The magenets allowed us to know how the ball was being squeezed.

A diagram showing how the Hall Effect Sensors worked.

The idea of this project was to have a regular stressball which could link to the users smart devices to automatically sooth with either lighting or music. We built a proof of concept when the users squeezes mapped to a color on a screen which would fade as the freqency of the squeezes slowed.
